# My Computer Engineering Journey – First Year Experience

Hello, I’m **Faizan Nouman** 👋  
This blog post reflects my beautiful journey as a **Computer Engineering student**, covering my memorable experiences from the **first day at university to my second semester**.

![University Journey Image](/assets/images_11zon.jpg)

---

## 1. First Day at University

My first day at university was full of happiness and excitement. As a student of **Computer Engineering**, everything felt new and interesting.

Our classes were being held in the **Electrical Department**, which made things a bit confusing for me on the first day.

![University Journey Image](/assets/images%20(1)_11zon.jpg)

When I reached the department, I found it difficult to locate my classroom. I felt a bit lost, but then I asked a senior student for help. He kindly guided me to the correct class, and that small act made me feel more comfortable in the new environment.

When I entered the classroom, it was very crowded because it was our **orientation day**. I quietly went to the last seat and sat next to a boy. We started talking, and that simple conversation turned into a great friendship.

Meeting a new friend on the very first day made my experience even more special.

As the orientation session began, I got the opportunity to meet my teachers and interact with them. It gave me a better understanding of my field and what to expect in my academic journey.

By the end of the day, I returned to my hostel room feeling tired but happy, as my first day had been full of new experiences, challenges, and memorable moments.

### Conclusion

My first day at university taught me that even if things seem confusing at the beginning, with a little help and confidence, everything becomes easier.

---

## 2. My Most Memorable Moment – Programming Fundamentals Lecture & Lab

One of the most memorable moments of my university journey was my very first **Programming Fundamentals lecture and lab**.

Our instructor, **Dr. Bilal**, introduced us to the course, and at first, I felt very worried and anxious.

I had never even heard the term **programming** before, and I wondered how I would ever understand this subject.

However, as the classes progressed, I gradually began to understand the fundamentals.

In the first semester, we learned **Python**, and with continuous practice and excellent teaching, programming became one of my strongest skills.

This experience taught me that even the most challenging subjects can become manageable with the right guidance and persistence.

---

## 3. My First University Exams – NETS Exams

As time passed, the first exams of my university life, the **NETS exams**, approached.

I was very anxious because it was my first experience with university-level exams.

Despite my fear, I trusted myself and worked hard to prepare.

When the results came, I was thrilled to see that my hard work had paid off.

I achieved excellent marks.

This moment taught me that **confidence and consistency lead to success**.

---

## 4. Departmental Cricket League – Balancing Sports and Studies

After the NETS exams, our university’s **Departmental Cricket League** began.

As someone passionate about cricket, I participated as a **bowler**.

![University Journey Image](/assets/WhatsApp%20Image%202026-04-05%20at%2010.31.53%20PM_11zon.jpg)

In my first match, I performed very well and helped our department secure a win.

Our team continued to perform brilliantly and successfully reached the **final**.

Despite having a quiz on the same day, I chose to represent my department in the final.

Thankfully, our team won the championship.

This experience taught me the importance of **teamwork, leadership, and balancing academics with extracurricular activities**.

---

## 5. Machine Learning Project – Overcoming Challenges

During our programming course, our instructor assigned us a **Machine Learning project**.

![University Journey Image](/assets/images_11zon%20(1).jpg)

He guided us throughout the process, which made the project much easier to complete.

However, the most challenging part for me was the **presentation**, because speaking in front of people made me nervous.

Still, I worked confidently with my group members.

We successfully built a high-quality machine learning model and presented it in class.

Our instructor was highly impressed, and we received excellent marks.

This experience boosted my **confidence, teamwork, and communication skills**.

---

## 6. Final Exams – Hard Work and Achievement

After completing our project presentations, it was time for the **final exams**.

I dedicated myself completely, studying day and night.

When the results came, I was delighted to see excellent marks.

I completed my first semester with **outstanding results**.

This moment reinforced the value of **hard work and dedication**.

---

## 7. Hostel Life – Learning Independence and Building Friendships

Living in the hostel during my first semester was a completely new experience.

It was my first time living away from home.

Initially, I felt nervous, but gradually I learned how to manage everything on my own.

![University Journey Image](/assets/WhatsApp%20Image%202026-04-05%20at%2010.31.57%20PM_11zon.jpg)

Hostel life taught me:

- **Time management**
- **Self-discipline**
- **Responsibility**
- **Independence**

The best part was making new friends.

Late-night study sessions, birthday celebrations, and shared meals made hostel life truly memorable.

---

## 8. Welcome Party – A Warm Start to University Life

One of the most exciting moments of my first semester was the **welcome party** organized for new students.

![University Journey Image](/assets/images%20(2)_11zon.jpg)

The hall was beautifully decorated with lights and banners.

Music, games, quizzes, and performances created a lively atmosphere.

It was a perfect opportunity to meet new classmates and seniors.

This event helped me feel comfortable and connected to university life.

---

## 9. End of First Semester & Start of Second Semester

At the end of the first semester, I received my results from the **Engineering Management System**.

I was overjoyed to see my performance.

After that, I went home and happily shared my achievements with my family.

When the second semester started, I returned refreshed and excited.

Soon, I attended:

- **Engineering conference**
- **FISHA event**
- **Department activities**

I represented the **Computer Engineering department** in the structural team.

This experience improved my:

- **Teamwork**
- **Leadership**
- **Event management skills**

During this time, **Ramadan** also began, making the semester even more memorable.

Sharing **Sehri and Iftar** with friends in the hostel was a beautiful experience.

---

## 10. Second Semester – Database Course and Online Learning

In the second semester, our same instructor started teaching us the **Database course**.

This made the whole class happy because we already admired his teaching style.

We learned:

- **Database design**
- **SQL**
- **Data management**
- **Schema concepts**

Later, due to unexpected war-related events, our classes shifted to **online learning**.

Although it was a sudden change, we adapted quickly.

This taught me an important lesson:

> Learning never stops, even in difficult circumstances.

It strengthened my **discipline, flexibility, and adaptability**.

---

# Final Thoughts

My Computer Engineering journey has been full of learning, growth, challenges, and beautiful memories.

From the first day at university to programming, cricket, hostel life, projects, and online learning — every moment has helped shape me into a better student and person.

This is just the beginning of my journey, and I am excited for what lies ahead 🚀
